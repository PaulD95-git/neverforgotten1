Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Creating a memorial is simple. Click on "Create Memorial" in the navigation menu, fill in the basic information about your loved one, and you can start adding photos, stories, and other memories. Our step-by-step guide will help you through the process.

Your memorial will remain online forever as part of our commitment to preserving memories. We offer permanent hosting with no expiration date, ensuring future generations can access and contribute to the memorial.

Each memorial automatically generates a unique QR code that links directly to the memorial page. You can download and print this code to include on memorial cards, headstones, or other keepsakes. When scanned with a smartphone camera, it will open the memorial directly.
