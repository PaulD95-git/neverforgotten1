From Personal Loss to Lasting Legacy

A Solo Developer's Journey

NeverForgotten was created after experiencing the profound loss of a loved one. I realized how quickly physical mementos can fade or be lost, and how difficult it is to preserve memories for future generations.

This platform was born from my personal journey through grief and my desire to create something meaningful from that pain. As a developer, I channeled my skills into building a digital space where memories could live on forever.

What started as a personal project to honor one life has grown into a platform helping thousands preserve their loved ones' legacies.
